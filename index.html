Our Promise to

Make a difference

Here at Quak-en we have a saying "take care of the earth and it will take care of you" and we invite you to join us in taking care of our wetlands. As it is now our wetlands are in danger of drying up, with local heatwaves, hunters damaging the ecosystems, and general human polution. We have partnered with the US EPA in protecting, restoring, and monitoring our wetlands. 20% of our profits directly go to the US EPA, we are doing our part, and we appreciate any help.

Our Story

Founded in May of 1990, we are a company based out of Verona, WI. Even before the offical establishment of the company we've had dedicated members making rubber ducks ever since the invention of them. And what better way to let everyone else enjoy them across the world than this. We always focus on our quality and durability of our products, and always ask our selfs one simple question, "who is the user for this product". We hope you enjoy our products as much as we do.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are the plastics in your product biodegradeable?

Our products are all created to be biodegradeable and safe for the enviroment. This allows for less waste in landfills, and incase you lose your rubber duck it wont affect the enviroment as bad as a traditional plastic duck would.

Are the plastics in your product animal safe?

Yes! No harmful plastics are used in our products! The enviroment is safe, along with all animals!

What portion of your company revenue is donated to charity?

Roughly 20% of our companies profits are donated to the US EPA, and various charities that support our ideals. This can range anywhere from $100,000-200,000/yr. Our year over year breakdown can be found at www.quak-en/yearly-profits
